Story

I was on a broken wooden board, lost in the ocean through storms and endless dark nights.
The waves shook me, and every shore I reached pushed me back.
The skies punished me with lightning, and the rain began to hurt.
I lost hope in this world, counting my end instead of dreams.
Then I heard a voice in the cold breeze, whispering my name - beautiful as ever.
I followed it with my heart. Each step softened the night, each breath felt less alone.
The wind stopped hurting; it began to guide.
There was a woman sitting by the seashore, calm as the moonlight. Love became my direction.
She reached out her hand, not to save me— but to choose me.
She pulled me from the sea, from the storms, from myself.
And at her feet, where the waves finally learned silence, I was not rescued— I was reborn.
